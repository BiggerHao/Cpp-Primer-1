Exercise 4.2: Using Table 4.12 (p. 166), parenthesize the following expressions
to indicate the order in which the operands are grouped:

	(a) * vec.begin()
	(b) * vec.begin() + 1

By Faisal Saadatmand

(a) * ((vec.begin)())
	Order of associativity: . () *
(b) ((* ((vec.begin)())) + 1)
	Order of associativity: . () * +

Simplified:
(a) *(vec.begin())
(b) (* (vec.begin())) + 1
