Exercise 3.13: How many elements are there in each of the following vectors?
What are the values of the elements?

	(a) vector<int> v1;
	(b) vector<int> v2(10);
	(c) vector<int> v3(10, 42);
	(d) vector<int> v4{10};
	(e) vector<int> v5{10, 42};
	(f) vector<string> v6{10};

By Faisal Saadatmand

(a) empty vector
(b) 10 elements, default initialized to 0.
(c) 10 elements, initialized to 42.
(d) 1 element, initialized to 10.
(e) 2 elements, initialized to 10 and 42.
(f) 10 elements, default initialized to empty strings.
