Exercise 7.20: When are friends useful? Discuss the pros and cons of using
friends.

By Faisal Saadatmand

They are useful when we need to allow a non-member function to access the
private members of a class. They provide an easy way to include non-member
functions in the interface of a class. The downside is that they could violate
the main principle of encapsulated, i.e. hiding the implementation, because
may require direct access private members.
